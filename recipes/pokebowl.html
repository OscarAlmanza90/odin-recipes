POKEBOWL

Ingredients:

1 pound of fresh raw fish (such as salmon or tuna), cut into small cubes 1 cup of cooked rice 1 avocado, sliced 1 small cucumber, sliced 1 small carrot, julienned 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced 2 tablespoons of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of sesame oil 1 teaspoon of rice vinegar 1 teaspoon of honey 1 clove of garlic, minced 1/2 teaspoon of grated ginger Sesame seeds, for garnish Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

Cook the rice according to package instructions and set aside. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, minced garlic, and grated ginger to make the dressing. Arrange the cooked rice in the bottom of a bowl. Top the rice with the cubed fish. Add the sliced avocado, cucumber, carrot, and red onion to the bowl. Pour the dressing over the ingredients in the bowl. Garnish with sesame seeds and fresh cilantro. Serve and enjoy!
